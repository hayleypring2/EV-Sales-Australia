Search cars

Search for a specific vehicle or compare several different vehicles and view a downloadable and shareable summary of data on emissions, fuel consumption, electric range, cost and noise data.

Vehicle variables

Vehicles that are the identical make and model produce different emissions and use different amounts of fuel depending on the vehicle body type, engine, transmision, and drivetrain. Please specify vehicle variables to view results.

Disclaimer

The fuel consumption and emissions data reported on this website is based on laboratory testing in controlled conditions and should only be used to compare vehicles. Your on-road fuel consumption and emissions will depend on the circumstances in which you operate the vehicle. For more guidance visit our guide to using tools and calculators.

An average total of consumption from urban and extra urban conditions.

Urban:

Measurement is taken at a low average speed (~19km/h) where conditions are typically stop and start.

Extra:

Measurement is taken at a high average speed (~63km/h) where conditions are typically at a constant speed.
